Emily Gonzalez

Photographer


My name is Emily Gonzalez, and I am a current first-year Spanish major at the University of California, Riverside. I graduated from Yucaipa High School in 2019 with highest honors, and during my time there, I actively participated in the Yearbook Publication and Photography course. For over two years, I was an editor and trained students to take and edit pictures of high enough quality to publish and print in the Yucaipa High annual yearbook. In my photography course, I shot on Nikon and earned how to utilize Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom to further enhance my photographs.

During the end of my Senior year of high schoolI decided to pursue employment in my hoppy and was hired by GradImages, a photography company dedicated to capture and disctribute the memories of thousands of high school graduations. In my first year, I was given the position of Photographic assistant, eventually leading my way up to Photographer. Throughout that experience, I developed skills in communication with my team, patience through long and uncomfortable hours of prepping and shooting, and dilligence during unfavorable and tough conditions.

Today, while I focus mainly on my academic studies, I still thoroughly enjoy taking and editing pictures in my free time, and look forward to the upcoming graduation season to pursue my photography professionally.
